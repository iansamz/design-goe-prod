Package Includes:

  • Three meals per day whilst on safari

  • Game entrance fees

Day 1: Nairobi – Masai Mara

Depart Nairobi in the morning and drive to Masai Mara for lunch at Mara Sidai Camp. Afternoon Masai village visit. Dinner and overnight at the camp.

Day 2: Masai Mara

Spend the whole day in the park including a visit to the Mara River and view of the Big Five of Africa. Picnic lunch, dinner and overnight at the camp.

Day 3: Masai Mara – Lake Nakuru

Leave Masai Mara after breakfast and proceed for game drive enroute to Lake Nakuru for lunch at Nakuru. Afternoon game drive, dinner and overnight.

Day 4: Lake Nakuru – Amboseli

Leave Lake Nakuru after breakfast and drive to Nairobi in time for early lunch. Later drive to Amboseli, game viewing. Arrive late in the day at Kimana Camp, dinner and overnight.

Day 5: Amboseli

Spend the day exploring (on game drive) this land of Kilimanjaro. All meals and overnight at the camp.

Day 6: Amboseli – Nairobi

Leave Amboseli after breakfast and drive through Masai country to Tsavo West through Chyulu Gate for lunch at Ngulia Lodge. Afternoon visit to the Rhino Sanctuary. Dinner and overnight.
