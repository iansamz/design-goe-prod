NAIROBI HALF DAY TOUR

ABOUT PACKAGE

A Nairobi excursion where you spend a wonderful half day at the historical Karen Blixen museum (Out of Africa) and with giraffes and baby elephants! Visiting the Giraffe Manor and the Daphne Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage-The David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust: A Haven for Elephants and Rhinos.

Destination Details

  1. Karen Blixen Museum - "I had a farm in Africa, at the foot of the Ngong Hills' ...... for those lovers of "Out of Africa" the museum will take you back in time to breathe the spirit of the writer and the lives she immortalized.
  2. The Giraffe Centre - This education center gives you an opportunity to get up close and personal with Rothchild giraffes.
  3. Daphne Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage - Get up close and personal with orphaned elephant (and often rhino). This is where Daphne Sheldrick has perfected the raising and re-integrating of orphaned elephants into the wild. (Open to the public from 11.00am - 12.00pm daily)
