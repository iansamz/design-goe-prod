5 DAY SAFARI PACKAGE

ABOUT PACKAGE

Maasai Mara, Lake Naivasha, Lake Bogoria and Amboseli Visitations

Number of Travellers Price Details

DAY 1: NAIROBI-MASAI MARA (Approximate 7hrs drive)

After breakfast early in the morning leave Nairobi or respectful hotel/airport at 8.30 am and travel through the The Great Rift Valley and have a view.
Lunch hour stop at the mall and grab some lunch at the mall.
Continue with safari to the Masai Mara and arrive there late in the afternoon at around 3.00pm.
Have a visit to the Masai village and learn of the Masai culture and custom.
Take a rest in the hotel from long journey.
Meal plan: Dinner

DAY 2: MASAI MARA

After breakfast at 8.30 am leave for a full day game drive at the national park in the jungle searching for the Big Five animals of Africa.
Lunch will be served at 1.00pm in the wild as a picnic lunch.
Have an afternoon game drive in the national park.
Meal plan: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner(FB)

DAY 3: MASAI MARA –LAKE NAIVASHA (approximate 4 hrs. and ½ drive)

After breakfast have a morning game drive in the national park to have a view of the beautiful sunrise from the African Horizon in the wild as you also view the hunting done by the king of the jungle “Simba” who’s the lion.
Return to camp by 8.30 am and pack belongings to head for the lake Naivasha
“Home of the Hippos”
Check in at the respectful hotel for lunch at Lake Naivasha.
Head for Hell’s Gate National park and do an afternoon bike game drive. Riding in the park with the animals is an awesome experience towards the gorges of hell’s gate.
Visit the Gorges and the hot showers of the scenery and also visit the crescent island in Lake Naivasha on boat rides to visit Hippo points.
Meal plan: Lunch and dinner.

DAY 4: NAIVASHA-LAKE BOGORIA/LAKE NAKURU

After breakfast, take out cameras and head for lake Bogoria to view the flamingo’s ad hot geysers/showers.
After the game drive return to the hotel in lake naivasha and have an overnight stay.
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner.

DAY 5: LAKE NAIVASHA-AMBOSELI NATIONAL PARK

Check-out from your lake Naivasha safari hotel after breakfast and head to Amboseli National Park via Nairobi to Emali, divert right to Amboseli reaching your safari camp/lodge in Amboseli in time for lunch. After lunch rest until 1600hrs the go out for the evening safari game-drive. On a clear day you will be able to see the snow of Mt Kilimanjaro, Gnus, Zebras, Lions and other animals.
Return to the Camp for dinner and overnight.

DAY 6: AMBOSELI NATIONAL PARK-NAIROBI

After breakfast, with your packed lunch venture into Amboseli national park for a half day safari game drive. This is a good opportunity to enjoy nature without any hurry. Take your time and enjoy, you will see the gnu, giraffe, gazelles and other animals. Observation Hill where you can have great panoramic views over the Amboseli plains.
Thereafter pack belongings and head back for Nairobi /hotel in respective points or airport drop off.
Meal plan: breakfast.
