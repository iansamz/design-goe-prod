ORIENTATION

Orientation is carried out by our Kenyan team in Nairobi. It begins on your arrival date and covers everything you need to know for your volunteer program in Kenya. Introduction to Kenya, Kenyan Customs, Language Training Details, Rules and Expectations, Safety, Travel Opportunities in Kenya, Introduction to your project and placement. The orientation will also give you a chance to meet other volunteers. Once your orientation is complete, you will be transported to your homestay and introduced to your host family.
